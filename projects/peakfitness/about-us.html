Peak Training | About Us

Our Story

We started our business in 2010. Always resided in Cary, NC. A family from whom some of our members were affected by the fast-paced and fast food culture we live in with diminishing health due to what we discovered to be lack of appropriate and fresh eating with a combination of all work and no play. Our owner father of 4 sons and daughters, decided to do something about it. After multiple efforts of research, they began implementing customized disciplinary methods of eating and exercising on a daily basis. Each member of our family supported each other with encouragement and customized organic diets and exercising. 2 years later, the owner reversed his heart and high blood pressure condition to 100% healthy! And of the sons and daughters, who suffered from gluten intolerant problems, obtained full recovery and continue a gluten free and organic diet in combination with exercise targeted to build longevity. From there evolved our business Peak Training.

Our Business And Service To You

  • We started our training facility in 2010. We reside in Cary, NC and provide peak quality holistic fitness programs for all families in Cary and the Triangle Area.
  • We aim to provide holistic fitness training to the local community of families, the elderly, and all those who seek a balance between staying fit, and eating healthy to keep it that way.
  • Our training facility encompasses organic diet programs with a combination of personal and class-based physical training for the goal of prevention against diseases due to inappropriate care of the body.
  • Our name is known in the local market, because we stand out from our competition in the professional resources our staff offers, such as on-site naturopathic physician consultation.
  • All nutritional consultation and programs provide our customers with local farm market resources for purchasing food for your nutrition program.
  • Peak Training has a track record of quality information and service that other fitness centers have used from us, time and time again.
