About

Web Designer and Front-End Developer in the Triangle Area of North Carolina


Hi there! I appreciate you taking a look at my web development portfolio site. I hope you enjoy looking at my projects as much as I did creating them. I am a web designer and Front-end Developer with 2 years of experience creating interfaces for websites in HTML5/CSS languages, and in WordPress and Joomla content managers. I hope there's some projects here that can demonstrate how I can be of help to your web development needs. I take delight in designing the interface and layout of digital content. I like the mix of visual elements coming together to catch attention from users it was meant for. And it doesn't stop there, I am very driven to ensure the content and interface of websites and apps I make, are accessible to as many users as possible.


Accessibility has been a more prominent phrase around the web design and development world, but it truly is important to ensure you are making your content available to as many users as possible, in additon to ensuring your website or web app meets Web Content Accessibility Guidelines : which is a requirement now for all digital content to be accessed through the web or internet. Finally, Search engines like Google will rank your site content at a higher priority in search results, if your content is more accessible than the competition's. It is a passion of mine to make websites and apps as Search Engine and User friendly as needed. I have been on the side of a user who wants to access great content, but the content was just not accessible: it was just not easy to find due to poor navigation, or color scheme made the context illegible.
